About Us

Mission Statement


The Heart of Hope Foundation is a relatively new, non-profit
organization founded in 2015. Our mission is to provide families with
psychological, moral and spiritual support.
Among its different activities, we have a free Grief Psychotherapy
Service for adults, adolescents and children who have suffered the loss
of a loved one and are going through mourning.


Main Programs


  1. Raising Aeareness of Domesic Violence
  2. Grief Psychotherapy Service

A Heart of Hope Foundation was founded by a group of people living in the United States and Lima, Peru. Awareness
of the violence that exists in this world (one of the founders was a victim of domestic abuse) touched our hearts and we
wanted to help these victims of abuse. We are aware of the immense need these people have for integral, fiscal,
intellectual, emotional, social, and professional help and support. A Heart of Hope Foundation is based out of Miami, Fl
and is registered as a non profit organization 501(C)(3). All contributions received are tax deductible charitable
donations. EIN: 47-3263632/ N15000001730. Violence against women and children is a crime against human dignity
and basic human rights. It is a tragedy that transcends all sectors of society and is not reliant on class, economic
status, culture, education level, age, race, ethnicity, or religion. Ending violence is essential for a good individual and
social development and the full participation in all areas of life. That is why we cannot just be spectators, we must act in
a constructive and positive way to help them exercise their rights and eliminate situations of violence that affect them
