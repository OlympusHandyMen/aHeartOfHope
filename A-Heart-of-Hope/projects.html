Grief Psychotherapy Service


Our organization provides families with psychological, moral and spiritual support.
Among its different activities, we have a free Grief Psychotherapy Service for adults, adolescents and children
who have suffered the loss of a loved one and are going through mourning.


If you have lost a loved one, it is possible for you to go through all kinds of emotions. You are likely to be sad, worried or
even scared. Maybe you were not prepared, and you feel quite confused by the loss. You may feel angry, guilty,
exhausted, or empty. Your emotions can be very confusing than normal, and you may have feelings you have never
experienced before. All these emotions are natural reactions to the death, loss of a loved one.

You are going through a grieving process, which is the process that follows the loss of a loved one. This loss is
definitive, but you can also mourn for other life events that don’t involve the death of a love one, such as the rupture of
a relationship, illness, involuntary absence of a loved one, etc.

Nobody is prepared for the loss of a loved one (parents, children, couples, siblings, etc.) we naturally lock ourselves in,
and lose interest in life; children are the most affected, they have many questions, that we can’t answer. The Heart of
Hope Foundation has a team of professionals to help these families free of charge. Part of the grieving process is to
recognize the pain that causes the loss of a loved one, however life goes on, and there are other members of the
family that still need us. We completely understand your pain.

We are aware that going to a private professional is extremely expensive, at A Heart of Hope Foundation, we have
experienced it in our own flesh, we know what it feels like to go through that painful experience. We know that with the
help of God and the team of professionals, we can move forward with our lives.

We are at your service, to give you comfort, love, and to help you restore your lives. A Heart of Hope Foundation!
Saving Lives, Restoring Families!


A Heart of Hope International Foundation, a nonprofit organization, was created to bring help to victims of domestic
abuse, holistically, physical, intellectually, emotionally, socially and professionally. Our mission is to impart knowledge
and principles that will restore and strengthen family relationships and to uplift victims of abuse into becoming useful
members of society. A Heart of Hope was created to equip women and their children with modern and effective tools,
where support is offered to women and their children who experience violence. We plan to set up a 24 hour hotline that
will provide counseling, legal advice and options to go to a shelter to find safety. This foundation aims to promote
growth and harmony in the family, providing care to women to promote a culture of non-violence and gender equality. In
the long term we plan to build a day center that will have classes and childcare available for women and their children.
Our after school program will help teach children of single mothers, not only basic life skills not commonly taugh in the
current school system (grooming, setting up a resume, cooking, cleaning, doing taxes, financial planning, etc) but will
also teach children to treat each other with kindness, respect, and empathy. Our goal is prevention. If a child grows up
in an abusive, mysoginistic home, that will be all he learns. At A Heart of Hope, we want to be the example of love,
respect, and compassion that every child should have growing up. Our day center will also be a place, like our hotline,
where women can come to find legal advice, counseling, and overall support in times of need. We will have various
classes available for women that will teach them job skills, resume building, interview skills, self worth, money
management, to help them get out of abusive codependent situations and feel confident enough to thrive on their own.
